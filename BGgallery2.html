My Blaze Pizza Experience

I initially joined Blaze Pizza as a temporary position, intending to keep afloat while exploring other opportunities that aligned more closely with my skills. However, under the leadership of Michael Ghrist, the district manager for the Tampa branch, I discovered new avenues for growth. Blaze Pizza not only provided me with valuable opportunities but also, through Mike’s faith and guidance, enabled me to elevate my artwork to a professional level and showcase my abilities to millions across Florida.

Blaze Pizza gave me opportunities I could never have imagined possible for someone in my position. More than anything else, they have taught me how to blaze my own trail.
